# Introduction

I try to expose some components that will easy your life, but with a minimal style.

Because I know you will be better than me at designing your components, I expose as much styling as
I can. So you will get some components that you will love, with YOUR design, but simple to use.

## Utilities

I included some utilities to get your life easier.

Here is an exhaustive list of them:

- [Color](/docs/color--page) to create and manipulate colors.
- [Colors](/docs/colors--amber) to get Material Colors easily e.g.,.: `Colors.red[500]`
  <div
    style={{
      width: 100,
      height: 30,
      display: 'inline-block',
      backgroundColor: Colors.red[500].toRGBA(),
    }}
  />
- [Icons](/docs/icons--default) to easily select Material Icon e.g.,.: `Icons.face`
  <Icon icon={Icons.face} />
